EPPS 6302 Assignment 01

  1. Analyze Survey:

  1. How is the survey structured? The survey begins with an introduction that is fairly incomplete. It should introduce the project more thoroughly but without biasing answers to the questions that follow. The intro is followed by 18 questions. The first 13 questions ask about the respondent’s preferences and behaviors regarding movies and software. The remaining questions ask about the respondent’s characteristics in terms of location, demographics, marital status and income.

  2. What is the questionnaire composed of? The questionnaire is composed of an introduction, a panel of statements to which the respondent can express degrees of agreement, numerous multiple choice questions, multiple yes/no questions, two questions asking the respondent to choose an expected cost, two questions that require a choice from a drop-down menu

  3. How are the questions ordered? The survey begins with an introduction, which is followed by 18 questions. The first 13 questions ask about the respondent’s preferences and behaviors regarding movies and software. The remaining questions ask about the respondent’s characteristics in terms of location, demographics, marital status and income.
